Event Description : “Film as dream, film as music. No art passes our conscience in the way film does, and goes directly to our feelings, deep down into the dark rooms of our souls.” -Ingmar Bergman. The essence of movie-making is using film’s own visual language to create meaning and the highest achievement of the cinematic form is fusing film and viewer into one. If you are passionate about it and have a capable team, then step up, participate this wonderful event and show us your PASSION WITH REELS!!!


Rules and Regulations

1.Must be a short film with a story.
2.Avoid political biasness, obscenity, pornography, excessive violence. In such case, the submission will be rejected.
3.Participants may or may not be from same college but submission of a copy of their college id card is compulsory.
4.The maximum number of team members can be 8 (including the cast).
5.The runtime for films should not exceed 10 minutes.
6.Films should not be copied from other sources.
7.Each team should have an appropriate title for the movie.
8.Each film should begin with a disclaimer, which should also include the event name, college name, the title of the movie and point of contact.
9.Submission of video should be done through CD, DVD or Pen drive. The movie should be playable on VLC & KM Player.
10.Movies should be in 720p(HD) minimum.
11.Movies that were submitted last year in Techstorm would not be accepted.
